# Main takeaways
1. Manage anxiety
	- Get out of your way
	- Accept that you are anxious it will help to not spiral
	- You don't have to be perfect
	- Try to be conversational and not a performance during presentations
	- Try to be in the present, you can take a small walk before the presentation, or music
	- Game: Look at objects and try to say some other name for that object
	- Look at it as an opportunity
	- Listen and then respond
	- Respond in stories
	- Structure is very important for communication
	- When you have to respond you have to figure out 2 things what to say and how to say it
	- How it say it is the structure
	- Two structures that can be helpful are problem/ oppertunity, solution and benefit and the other is what, so what, now what
	- you can try to paraphrase, this can help you buy some time to respond and also makes the other person feel heard
