
### SRE 1 (Junior SRE)

**Responsibilities:**

1. **Monitoring and Incident Response:**
    - Monitor system performance and availability.
    - Respond to incidents and outages under the guidance of senior SREs.
    - Participate in on-call rotations.
2. **System Maintenance:**
    - Perform routine system maintenance tasks.
    - Apply patches and updates as directed.
    - Assist in the deployment of new applications and services.
3. **Documentation and Reporting:**
    - Document incidents, resolutions, and system configurations.
    - Report on system performance and incidents to senior team members.
4. **Learning and Development:**
    - Engage in training and development programs.
    - Learn and understand the tools and technologies used by the SRE team.
    - Follow best practices for security, performance, and reliability.
5. **Collaboration:**
    - Work closely with senior SREs to understand system architecture and workflows.
    - Assist in cross-functional team projects as needed.
  7. **Infrastructure Management:**
	- Assist in the provisioning and maintenance of development, testing, and production environments.
	- Apply updates and patches as directed by senior DevOps engineers.
	- Monitor infrastructure performance and health
8. **CI/CD Pipelines:**
    - Help set up and maintain Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipelines.
    - Troubleshoot and resolve build and deployment issues.
9.  **Automation:**
    - Implement simple automation scripts to improve efficiency.
    - Learn and use configuration management tools like Ansible, Puppet, or Chef.

### SRE 2 (Mid-level SRE)

**Responsibilities:**

1. **Incident Management:**
    - Lead incident response efforts and coordinate with relevant teams.
    - Analyze and resolve complex issues related to system reliability and performance.
    - Develop and implement incident response procedures.
2. **System Optimization:**
    - Identify and address performance bottlenecks and reliability issues.
    - Optimize system architecture for better performance and scalability.
    - Automate repetitive tasks to improve efficiency.
3. **Project Management:**
    - Manage and execute medium to large-scale projects.
    - Collaborate with development teams to integrate reliability into the development lifecycle.
    - Conduct root cause analysis and drive continuous improvement initiatives.
4. **Mentorship and Training:**
    - Mentor and guide junior SREs (SRE 1) in their development.
    - Conduct training sessions on best practices and new technologies.
    - Review and provide feedback on the work of junior SREs.
5. **Documentation and Standards:**
    - Develop and maintain documentation standards.
    - Ensure accurate and comprehensive documentation of systems and processes.
6. **Automation and Scripting:**
	- Develop automation scripts and tools to streamline processes.
	- Implement Infrastructure as Code (IaC) using tools like Terraform or CloudFormation.
	- Automate repetitive tasks and processes.
7. **Security and Compliance:**
	- Implement security best practices in the deployment and infrastructure management processes.
	- Ensure compliance with organizational and regulatory standards.
	- Conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.

### SRE 3 (Senior SRE / Lead SRE)

**Responsibilities:**

1. **Strategic Planning:**
    
    - Develop and implement SRE strategies and initiatives.
    - Drive the adoption of SRE principles and practices across the organization.
    - Set goals and objectives for the SRE team.
2. **Architecture and Design:**
    
    - Design and architect complex, large-scale, and highly reliable systems.
    - Lead the evaluation and implementation of new technologies.
    - Ensure systems are designed for high availability, scalability, and disaster recovery.
3. **Leadership and Mentorship:**
    
    - Provide technical leadership and direction to the SRE team.
    - Mentor and develop the skills of mid-level and junior SREs.
    - Foster a culture of continuous improvement and learning.
4. **Incident and Problem Management:**
    
    - Oversee major incident response and coordinate with executive stakeholders.
    - Lead post-incident reviews and drive systemic improvements.
    - Develop and enforce incident management and escalation protocols.
5. **Collaboration and Communication:**
    
    - Act as a liaison between the SRE team and other departments.
    - Communicate effectively with executive leadership about system reliability and performance.
    - Represent the SRE team in cross-functional projects and initiatives.
6. **Innovation and Automation:**
    
    - Drive innovation within the team by exploring new tools, technologies, and methodologies.
    - Lead efforts to automate processes and reduce manual intervention.
    - Advocate for and implement industry best practices.
