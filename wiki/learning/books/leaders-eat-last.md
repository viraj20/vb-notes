## The book's broader frame

Leaders Eat Last extends [[start-with-why|Start With Why]]'s WHY into team biology: oxytocin, cortisol, the circle of safety. The core claim is that leadership is about **extending safety to your people**, not consuming it. When people feel safe, the team's intelligence compounds; when they don't, individual self-preservation dominates and the team fragments.
