# Thirteen Reasons Why (Asher)

Parent: [[../index|Learning MOC]]

YA novel by Jay Asher. VB captured only 2 highlights; this is a **placeholder**.

## What was captured

> "You can hear rumors, but you can't know them." (loc 669) — distinction between secondhand story and lived truth.

> "Unlike old age or cancer, no one anticipates a suicide. They simply left without a chance to get things in order." (loc 738) — on the untimeliness of suicide vs. foreseen death; contrasts with [[tuesdays-with-morrie|Morrie]]'s weeks-of-preparation death.
