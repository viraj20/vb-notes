# How to Win Friends and Influence People (Carnegie)

- Author: Dale Carnegie
- ASIN: B07D17NY8N

## What was captured

> "God himself, sir, does not propose to judge man until the end of his days." — loc 454

Carnegie uses this to frame his principle: **don't criticize, condemn, or complain**. Judgment comes cheap; patience costs something. The book's broader thesis is that human relationships scale on sincere interest, genuine appreciation, and making the other person feel important — not on cleverness or persuasion.
