# And Every Morning the Way Home Gets Longer and Longer (Backman)

Parent: [[../index|Learning MOC]]

Fredrik Backman's novella about a grandfather slipping into dementia, told from inside his fading mind. VB captured 3 highlights; this is a **placeholder**.

## What was captured

- **Fear of ageing > fear of dying** (loc 77) — "Humans are a strange breed in the way our fear of getting old seems to be even greater than our fear of dying." Contrast with [[tuesdays-with-morrie|Morrie]], who reframes ageing as growth, not decay.
- **Failure is giving up** (loc 277) — "The only time you've failed is if you don't try once more."
- **On who laughs** (loc 286) — "All grown-ups are angry, it's just children and old people who laugh."
