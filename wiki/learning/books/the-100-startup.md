## Broader frame

Guillebeau's thesis: low-capital micro-businesses — the kind you can launch for $100 or less — are a practical alternative to the traditional jobs-and-stocks path. A small, profitable, self-owned business beats a speculative one almost every time because the feedback loops are fast and the downside is bounded.
